PocketDocs – User Data Deletion

Last updated: 28 November 2025

This page explains how you can request deletion of your PocketDocs account and related personal data (including data collected via Google Login or Facebook Login).

1. Delete your account from inside the app (when available)

In future versions of the PocketDocs app there will be an in-app option to delete your account and data. When this feature is available, you will be able to:

  • Open the PocketDocs app.
  • Go to Settings → Account → Delete account.
  • Confirm that you want to permanently delete your account and data.

After confirming, your account and associated personal data stored in Firebase (such as your profile and onboarding information) will be deleted or anonymised within a reasonable time.

2. Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app or if the in-app delete option is not available on your device, you can request deletion by contacting us directly.

Please send an email to:

pocketdocs0@gmail.com

Include the following information in your email:

  • The subject line: “PocketDocs – Delete my account”.
  • The email address you used to log in to PocketDocs (Google or Facebook account email).
  • Optional: any additional information that helps us identify your account (for example, the name shown in the app).

We will verify your request and delete or anonymise your personal data stored in our systems (Firebase Authentication, Firestore, etc.) within a reasonable timeframe, usually within 30 days, unless we are required to keep certain data for legal or security reasons.

3. What data will be deleted

When your account is deleted, we aim to remove the following types of data:

  • Your authentication record (Google/Facebook login) in Firebase Authentication.
  • Your user profile document in Firestore (name, email, onboarding information, interests, targets and similar fields stored by the app).
  • Any other personal information that can reasonably be linked to your account.

Some aggregated or anonymised usage statistics may be kept for analytics and service improvement, but these will no longer be linked to you personally.

4. Questions

If you have any questions about data deletion or your privacy rights, please contact us at
pocketdocs0@gmail.com.
